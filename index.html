FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Is this against Google's terms of service?
No. We ask your customers for reviews. We never fake reviews, pay for them, or filter by expected rating. Asking happy customers to share their experience is 100% within Google's guidelines — it's what Google themselves recommends.
How do customers receive the request?
We send a personalized email (and optionally SMS) 24 hours after the job is marked complete. It reads like it came directly from you — warm, human, personal. No generic blast. Your customers won't feel spammed.
How long until I see results?
Most contractors see their first new reviews within the first week. Meaningful rating improvement typically shows within 30–60 days depending on job volume. The more customers you have, the faster it works.
What if a customer leaves a bad review?
We alert you immediately when a 1-star review appears so you can respond fast. Responding to negative reviews professionally is the #1 way to protect your reputation. We catch them the moment they go live.
Can I cancel?
Yes, anytime. No annual contracts, no cancellation fees. Email us and you're done. But honestly — once you see the reviews coming in, you won't want to.
